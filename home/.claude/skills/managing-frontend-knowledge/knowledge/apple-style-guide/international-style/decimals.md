# Decimals

Guidelines for formatting decimal numbers in Apple materials.

## Decimal Separators

### Period (.)
Used in:
- United States
- United Kingdom
- Many Asian countries
- Example: 3.14159

### Comma (,)
Used in:
- Most European countries
- Latin America
- Example: 3,14159

## Thousands Separators

### Comma (,)
- US and UK format: 1,234,567.89

### Period (.) or Space
- European format: 1.234.567,89 or 1 234 567,89

## Regional Considerations

- Use appropriate separators for target locale
- Be consistent within a document
- Consider using spaces as thousand separators in international documentation
- Clarify format in technical specifications

## Best Practices

- Specify formatting conventions when ambiguity might exist
- Use locale-specific formatting in localized content
- Consider international standards (ISO 31-0) for technical documentation
- Test numerical displays across different locales
