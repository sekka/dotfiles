# Dates and Times

Guidelines for formatting dates and times in Apple materials.

## Date Formats

### US Format
- Month-Day-Year: June 9, 2025
- Numeric: 6/9/2025 (MM/DD/YYYY)

### International Formats
- Day-Month-Year: 9 June 2025
- Numeric: 09/06/2025 (DD/MM/YYYY)
- ISO 8601: 2025-06-09 (YYYY-MM-DD)

## Time Formats

### 12-Hour Format
- 3:30 PM
- 11:45 AM
- Include AM/PM designation

### 24-Hour Format
- 15:30
- 23:45
- Common in many countries outside the US

## Best Practices

- Use ISO 8601 format for technical documentation
- Specify time zones when relevant
- Use locale-appropriate formats for regional content
- Avoid ambiguous date formats (use month names instead of numbers when possible)
- Include day of week when helpful for context

## Time Zones

- Use standard time zone abbreviations (PST, EST, GMT, UTC)
- Convert times appropriately for global audiences
- Indicate whether time is local or in specific zone
