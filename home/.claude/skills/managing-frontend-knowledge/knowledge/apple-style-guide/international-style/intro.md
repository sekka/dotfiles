# International Style - Introduction

Guidelines for global communication in Apple materials.

## Overview

The International style section covers practical guidelines for creating content that works across different regions and cultures, including:
- Countries
- Currency
- Dates and times
- Decimals
- Languages
- Telephone numbers
- Units of measure

## Purpose

These guidelines ensure:
- Cultural appropriateness in global markets
- Consistency across localized materials
- Proper formatting for regional conventions
- Accessibility for international audiences

## Key Principles

- Respect regional conventions and preferences
- Use locale-appropriate formatting
- Consider cultural context in examples
- Maintain clarity across translations
