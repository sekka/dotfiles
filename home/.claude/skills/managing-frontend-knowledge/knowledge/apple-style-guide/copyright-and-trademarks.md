# Copyright and Trademarks

Guidelines for using copyright notices and trademarks in Apple materials.

## Copyright Notices

### Format
- © [Year] Apple Inc. All rights reserved.
- Use copyright symbol (©) not (c) or "Copyright"
- Include current year or year range
- Place at bottom of documents or in legal sections

### Best Practices
- Update copyright year annually
- Include company name exactly as trademarked
- Follow legal department guidance for specific materials
- Don't remove or alter existing copyright notices

## Trademark Usage

### Apple Trademarks
- Always use exact spelling and capitalization
- Don't use possessive or plural forms of trademarks
- Use trademark symbols appropriately (® or ™)
- Examples: iPhone®, Mac®, Apple Watch®

### First Use
- Include trademark symbol on first prominent use
- Subsequent uses may omit symbol in running text
- Include trademark attribution in legal section

### Third-Party Trademarks
- Respect other companies' trademarks
- Use proper capitalization and spelling
- Include appropriate attribution when required
- Don't imply endorsement or affiliation without permission

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Don't use trademarks as verbs or generic terms
- Don't modify or abbreviate trademarked names
- Don't use trademarks in domain names without authorization
- Don't create composite marks combining trademarks

## Attribution Examples

**Apple Products:**
"iPhone, Mac, and Apple Watch are trademarks of Apple Inc., registered in the U.S. and other countries."

**Third-Party:**
"[Product name] is a trademark of [Company name]."
